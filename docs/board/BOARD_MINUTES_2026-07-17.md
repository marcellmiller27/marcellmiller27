## 1. Strategic objective — Audience-first, institutional-grade, conversion-driven (RESOLVED)
- **RESOLVED — Core objective:** *Know our Targeted Audience.* JHI performs due diligence to
  **follow institutional-grade standards as practiced by our peers in the industry.**
- **Goal:** **conversion into new, long-term subscriptions** — earning subscribers who are
  seeking **solutions to the pain-points that drag/lag their business decisions**, delivered
  in a **timely manner**.
- **Operating implication:** the platform's language, structure, and outputs must feel native
  to institutional PE/M&A practitioners, advisors, and search funders — so there is **no
  confusion and no missed opportunities**.

## 2. Nomenclature policy — the MIRROR PRINCIPLE (RESOLVED)
- **RESOLVED:** JHI **mirrors the institutional-grade language of industry peers verbatim**
  (Mergr as the benchmark case study). **No invented terminology.**
- **Rationale (recorded):** the target audience already speaks this jargon → standard terms
  give **zero learning curve, instant credibility, and no confusion**; invented terms create
  hesitation and **lost conversions**. Language is a **conversion & trust decision**, not
  style.
- **Outliers:** genuine JHI-only capabilities (integrated financials/valuation, macro, SEC
  filings, SMB/search-fund diligence — Deal X-Ray/QoE/Valuation/Risk Score) are **discussed
  as a team** and framed **in the peers' language style**, never invented unilaterally.

## 3. Foundation & process discipline (RECORDED)
- **Foundation-first:** structure, IA/navigation, and nomenclature are settled **before**
  building features. Every PR is a **draft reviewed and approved by the Founder** (see
  `docs/ENGINEERING_POLICY.md`).
- Supporting record: the four-session Mergr dissection and the IA/nomenclature work are
  captured in `docs/PLATFORM_IA_BLUEPRINT.md` (PR #78), including the two-sector product
  model, entity/relationship graph, record templates, tool patterns, differentiation, data
  sourcing, and the nomenclature master list with disclosed definitions.

## 4. Differentiation reaffirmed (RECORDED)
- JHI goes beyond the benchmark via **integrated financials/macro on every record**, the
  **Deal X-Ray ↔ Buyer Match** two-sided loop, and the **SMB / search-fund bridge** (SDE,
  SBA, DSCR) that peers do not serve — all aimed at removing customer pain-points quickly.
