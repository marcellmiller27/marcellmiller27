# JHI Company Book — Policy, Procedures & Processes

> JHI-SIG: 69M2705M · JHI Research & Analytics Firm, Inc. (proprietary)
> **The single source of truth for how the firm operates.** Living document — every new
> capability is documented here as **Policy → Procedure → Process → Job Aid** before it is
> considered done.

## Guiding philosophy
- **"How we do anything is how we do everything."** Standards are universal, not situational.
- **Run live by Efficiency & Effectiveness.** *Efficiency* = do it with the least wasted
  energy; *Effectiveness* = do the right thing well. We document processes so we never spin
  our wheels re-solving solved problems.
- **Foundation first.** Structure and standards precede feature work.

## Definitions (so we use the words precisely)
| Term | Meaning |
|---|---|
| **Policy** | The **rule and the why** — a standing decision that governs behavior. |
| **Procedure** | The **step-by-step "how"** to perform a specific task correctly. |
| **Process** | The **end-to-end flow** connecting procedures/steps across the firm. |
| **Job Aid** | A **quick-reference** for a single task, incl. troubleshooting. |

## How we document (the meta-procedure) — MANDATORY
For **every** capability, feature, or recurring task, we produce/one-update:
1. **Policy** — the governing rule (if new governance is implied).
2. **Procedure** — the exact steps to do it.
3. **Process** — where it fits in the end-to-end flow.
4. **Job Aid** — a quick reference + troubleshooting (in `docs/TroubleShoot/Job-Aids/`).
Nothing is "done" until it is documented here (or linked from here).
