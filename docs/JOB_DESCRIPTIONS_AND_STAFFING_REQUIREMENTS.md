# John Henry Investments Job Descriptions and Staffing Requirements

## Purpose

This document defines recommended job descriptions, departments, staffing levels, skill requirements, education or experience requirements, legal personnel requirements, professional firm requirements, and other staffing needs for the John Henry Investments platform.

This is a planning document, not employment, legal, tax, compensation, or HR advice. Final hiring requirements should be reviewed with HR counsel, employment counsel, tax advisors, and compliance counsel.

## Staffing philosophy

The platform should not hire every role immediately. Staffing should follow revenue, risk, customer volume, and regulatory complexity.

Recommended staffing sequence:

1. Founder-led controlled beta with contractors.
2. Lean paid MVP team with outside legal/compliance and fractional finance.
3. Growth team with in-house engineering, customer success, compliance operations, and sales.
4. Scale team with formal departments.
5. Enterprise organization with legal, compliance, security, data, finance, sales, support, and product teams.

## Skill level definitions

| Level | Typical experience | Scope |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Intern / Assistant | 0-2 years | Administrative, research, support, documentation, supervised tasks |
| Associate / Junior | 1-3 years | Executes defined tasks with guidance |
| Specialist / Mid-level | 3-6 years | Owns workstreams, makes recommendations, manages limited scope |
| Senior | 6-10 years | Owns major systems, projects, customers, or compliance functions |
| Lead / Manager | 8-12 years | Leads people or cross-functional programs |
| Director | 10-15 years | Owns department-level strategy, budget, and execution |
| VP / Executive | 12-20+ years | Owns company-level outcomes, risk, revenue, or operations |
| Fractional / Advisor | 10-25+ years | Specialized guidance without full-time employment |

## Education versus experience policy

Recommended hiring policy:

```text
Accept equivalent experience for most technical, support, sales, and operations roles.
Require formal credentials only where law, accounting, security, compliance, or enterprise customers require them.
```

Roles that may require formal credentials:

- Attorney: JD and active bar license.
- CPA/controller/auditor: CPA preferred or required depending on scope.
- Compliance officer: securities/fintech compliance experience; Series licenses may be relevant if advisory activity is regulated.
- Security lead: CISSP, CISM, SOC 2, cloud security, or equivalent experience preferred.
- HR/payroll: employment law and payroll compliance experience preferred.

## Legal and compliance

### Required outside legal firm profile

John Henry Investments should engage a law firm or legal team with experience in:

- Fintech SaaS
- Securities/investment adviser analysis
- Financial disclaimers and marketing review
- AI product risk and disclaimers
- Privacy and data protection
- Bank/accounting/vendor API integrations
- Subscription terms and payment terms
- B2B enterprise contracts
- Intellectual property ownership
- Employment/contractor agreements

Recommended firm type:

```text
A fintech/startup law firm with securities regulatory capability, privacy/data protection capability, SaaS commercial contracts capability, and access to specialist counsel for investment adviser questions.
```

If one firm cannot cover all needs, use:

1. Startup/SaaS corporate counsel.
2. Securities/investment adviser regulatory counsel.
3. Privacy/data protection counsel.
4. Employment counsel.
5. IP/trademark counsel.

### Outside General Counsel

- JD and active bar license.
