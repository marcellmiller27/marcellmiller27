## Mandate
Bring **depth** and surface **unforeseen opportunities across all asset classes** —
equities, fixed income / credit, real assets (real estate, commodities), private markets
(business acquisition / PE), and digital assets — with an institutional, analyst voice.

## Product line (newsletter types)
| Type | Cadence | Purpose |
| --- | --- | --- |
| **Updates** | Recurring (e.g., weekly) | The standing brief on the economy and markets (e.g., "The Economic Brief"). |
| **Insider Briefs** | As-warranted | Deep-dive analysis on a theme, sector, or target — the "depth" mandate. |
| **Red Alerts** | Time-sensitive | Concise, high-priority signals when a threshold trips (rate move, dislocation, event risk). |
| **Opportunity Scans** | Recurring / triggered | Cross-asset idea generation — surfacing unforeseen opportunities. |

## Sourcing & governance
- **Today:** public data only — Federal Reserve (FRED), U.S. BLS, BEA, Treasury, World
  Bank, IMF, OECD, and market feeds. No licensed-vendor footprint.
- **When licensed data is added (e.g., Nasdaq Data Link / Sharadar):** editions consume
  **derived-only** metrics — non-reversible and non-substitutive — with the licensed raw
  data isolated server-side (see `docs/legal/nasdaq/FOUNDER_RESOLUTION_2026-07-20.md`).
  Confirm any mandatory-attribution and external-distribution/end-user-cap terms before use.
- **Voice:** independent JHI professional perspective; research/education only, never
  personalized advice; every edition carries the standard disclaimer.
